About me

I am a data lover who enjoy solving problems!

When I was a kid, I wanted to study the stars, so I studied astrophysics. For 10+ years, I work as a researcher at university. My research focused on the the relation between active supermassive black holes (AGN) and star formation in galaxies. In particular, I studied the physical conditions of the cold molecular gas in galaxies to establish whether it is forming stars and, if not, understand what is preventing star formation.

I currently transitioning from a successful academic career to the industry or the charity sector. I am an experienced data scientist with a strong background in big data analytics, data processing, spatial analysis and remote sensing, quantitative and predictive modeling, and machine learning. I also have strong communication skills, both spoken and written.
